About Sneaky Fun

Embark on the journey of creating Sneaky Fun, a classic snake game with a sneaky twist. Inspired by the love for retro games and a desire for a unique gaming experience, Sneaky Fun is the perfect blend of nostalgia and innovation. This project is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School.
